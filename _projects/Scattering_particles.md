### Overview

This project explored several fundamental phenomena in nuclear and particle physics.

Topics investigated include:

- Rutherford alpha-particle scattering and differential cross-sections
- Quantum tunneling of alpha particles using the WKB approximation
- Particle Induced X-ray Emission (PIXE)
- Ionization cross-sections and stopping power calculations
- Comparison of proton and muon interactions in matter
